The Philosophy

I’m John from Nigeria. I love calm, intentional, premium web design — the kind that builds trust and makes people want to buy without feeling pushy.

I have a strong sense of UX: how layout guides attention, where trust signals matter, what makes something feel high-end vs cheap, and how to reduce friction in the buying flow. I don’t write code line by line — I guide AI with very specific prompts to output clean Tailwind/HTML pages that feel hand-crafted. Still improving every day, but excited to help brands get pages that feel right.
